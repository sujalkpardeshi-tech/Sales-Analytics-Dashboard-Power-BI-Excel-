Business Intelligence Insights
📊 Revenue Distribution
All four regions are remarkably balanced — South leads at ₹1.28Cr (26.4%), followed closely by North, East, and West. No single region dominates, suggesting healthy geographic diversification without over-reliance on one market.
🏆 Category Leader
Technology drives 38.3% of total revenue (₹1.86Cr), outperforming both Furniture (32.8%) and Office Supplies (28.9%). Technology also holds the highest absolute profit at ₹32.9L — making it the star category worth prioritizing.
👥 Customer Segment
Consumer segment leads in revenue at ₹1.73Cr (35.5%) with 1,449 orders. However, Home Office customers generate ₹11,830 per order — slightly higher than Consumer (₹11,925) and Corporate (₹11,291), indicating B2B uplift potential.
📦 Product Stars
Monitor & Keyboard are the top 2 revenue generators at ₹64.3L and ₹57.8L respectively. Together they account for nearly 25% of total revenue. These two SKUs should anchor procurement, bundling, and promotional strategies.
📅 Seasonality Signal
Revenue spikes are visible in Aug–Sep and Mar across years. August 2023 hit ₹20.3L — the single highest month. Mid-year dips in Feb and July are consistent. Targeted campaigns before these peaks could yield 10–15% lift.
⚠️ Margin Consistency
Profit margin holds steady at ~17.4–17.6% regardless of discount level (0% vs 1–20%). This suggests discounting has not meaningfully eroded margins — but also means discounts aren't volume-compensating. Review discount strategy ROI carefully.
