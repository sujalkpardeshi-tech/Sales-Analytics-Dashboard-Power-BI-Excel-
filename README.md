# Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI-Excel-
Overview : This project showcases a Sales Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI, designed to provide actionable insights into sales performance across categories, products, regions, and time periods. It helps stakeholders track revenue trends, identify top-performing products, and analyze declines with clear visualizations.

Key Highlights : 1) Total Sales: ₹7M across 647 orders (Jan–Sep 2025).
                 2) Category Performance: Steel contributes the largest share (≈59) And Alloys (≈22%) and Refractory                                                 (≈19%) follow.
                 3) Top Products: Product 8, Product 27, Product 37, Product 22, and Product 10 drive the highest sales
                 4) Quarterly Trends: Q1 strong with Steel dominance.Q3 shows decline in Refractory sales (highlighted in                                        “Explanation of Decrease”).
                 5) Revenue Decline Analysis: Clear breakdown of increases, decreases, and other adjustments across Q3                        and Q4.

Features : 1)Category-wise performance with average sales per category and Top 5 products by sales revenue.
           2)Quarterly and monthly breakdowns for trend analysis.
           3)Regional insights (North region sample).
           4)Revenue decline analysis to explain dips in performance                 

Visuals to Include :  Bar chart: Sales by Category
                      Line chart: Sales by Month
                      Pie chart: Category contribution %
                      Table: Top 5 Products by Sales
                      Decline analysis: Q3/Q4 breakdown
